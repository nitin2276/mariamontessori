When can I register my child?

You should aim to register your child as soon as possible. Most good schools do not have places available immediately and they maintain a waiting lists, so it is best in your interest to register a place for child as soon as possible.

Do you accept childcare vouchers?

Yes we do accept childcare vouchers. We are registered with most of the Childcare Voucher providers.

Are you part of Goverment Funding Scheme?

Yes we are a registered with Ealing Council for Early Years Grants (Government funding). Under this scheme all 3 and 4 years are eligible for 15 hour per week of Government funding. You could even be eligible for 30 hours government funding per week.

We also accept enrolment for 2 year old funded children.

How do I check my eligibility for 30 hours government funding?

What is the procedure to apply for admission?

We encourage all prospoective parents to book an appointment for visiting us during our operation working hours and see us in action. Viewing slot is generally booked for half an hour. Once you have decided, you would be required to submit filled-in registration form. Upon your submission, you would be notified whether we can offer a place to your child immediately or your application has been put in waiting list.
