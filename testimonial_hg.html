Fantastic preschool. Our daughter has loved attending this nursery. The staff are kind, caring and highly trained. They teach the children to be independent (washing up their bowls after eating and putting toys away etc), and prepare them for starting school. Great regular activities such as forest school sessions, yoga and cooking were very much enjoyed. Parent portal allowed us to see lovely photos and feedback so we could see what our daughter had been doing each week.

Jamie Malik

Such a wonderful, welcoming and caring environment for small children. I researched and visited many nurseries in the local area, and Maria Montessori in Hale Gardens stood out as far and away the best. Their nurturing approach to play and education as part of the Montessori system reassured me that my daughter was in safe surroundings. The staff are incredible, and very supportive to nervous children (and parents!). Highly recommend.

Tricia Howey

Excellent Montessori. I tried quite a few others but found this the best. Good sized facility and genuinely concerned teachers who invest a lot in each child. My child attended for 1 year before we had to leave for overseas, but I was happy throughout with the care and education. The children are taught in a stimulating yet relaxed environment. The teachers are calm and professional. My daughter enjoyed her time there and I highly recommend this institution.

Sidra Khan

A well organised Montessori nursery in a good safe setting with an outdoor space. The teachers are caring, the teaching methods applied are effective and all the children seem happy and well settled.

Julia Dean

A wonderful, nurturing place of learning and of fun. As a nervous first time mother I was apprehensive when sending my 2.5 year old daughter to preschool, but I need not have worried. In a lovely setting near Ealing Common, this school is an ideal start to any little persons education. Caring, empathetic long serving staff put parents and pupils immediately at ease, and bring the children on a tremendous social, cultural & educational journey.

Ealing Common Rep
